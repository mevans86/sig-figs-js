This calculator takes significant digits into account and applies the rules of significant figures to calculations. It uses an expression parser developed originally by Silent Matt as well as my SigFloat Javascript object (see sig-figs.js in this directory).

Unitary functions: sin(a), cos(a), tan(a), asin(a), acos(a), atan(a), sqrt(a), log(a), abs(a), ceil(a), floor(a), round(a), exp(a), random(a), fac(a)

Binary functions: pyt(a, b), pow(a, b) or a ^ b, min(a, b), max(a, b), a % b, atan2(a, b)
